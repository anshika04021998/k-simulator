Intrinsic Motivation k(m, a) Simulator

Explore how monitoring intensity and autonomy jointly shape intrinsic motivation across four experimental treatment conditions.

⚙ Parameters

k₀ — Baseline motivation 0.50
Intrinsic motivation when m=0, a=0
α — Autonomy boost 0.40
Direct lift to k when autonomy a=1
β₁ — Monitoring lift 0.30
Positive direct effect of monitoring on k
β₂ — Crowding-out 0.60
Strength of monitoring's quadratic crowding-out
m_high — High monitoring 0.80
m_low — Low monitoring 0.20
Model equation
k(m, a) = k₀ + α·a

          − β₂·(1−a)·m²
